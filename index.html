What You Will Be Profiled On

  1. Self-Awareness Recognising your emotions and their effect on others.
  2. Self-Management Regulating impulses and adapting under pressure.
  3. Social Awareness Reading the emotional and political dynamics of a room.
  4. Relationship Management Influencing, developing and managing conflict with others.
About this instrument This is a pedagogical tool designed for in-class self-reflection and discussion. It is not a validated diagnostic instrument and should not be used to make hiring, promotion or clinical decisions. For research-grade assessment, see the ESCI (Hay/Korn Ferry) or EQ-i 2.0 (MHS).
